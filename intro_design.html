Frequently Asked Questions

What is design, and why is it important?

Design is the process of creating solutions to problems or challenges through visual means. It is important because it plays a crucial role in shaping the way we interact with the world around us, from products and services to digital experiences and communication.

Do I need any prior experience in design or art to enroll in this course?

No prior experience in design or art is required. This course is suitable for beginners with a passion for creativity and a desire to learn about design principles and techniques.

What software and tools will I need for this course?

Depending on the specific modules covered in the course, you may need access to design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Sketch, or Figma. However, alternatives and free software options will also be discussed.

What career opportunities are available in the field of design?

Design offers a wide range of career opportunities in areas such as graphic design, web design, UI/UX design, motion graphics and animation, branding and identity design, print design, and more.
