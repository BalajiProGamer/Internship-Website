Course Overview

Cloud computing is the on-demand availability of computer system resources, especially data storage and computing power, without direct active management by the user.It is the delivery of computing services —including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and intelligence—over the internet (“the cloud”) to offer faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.

Who is this course designed for?

This course is designed for IT professionals, developers, architects, and anyone interested in mastering advanced concepts and techniques in cloud computing.

While there are no strict prerequisites, a basic understanding of cloud computing fundamentals and experience with IT concepts would be beneficial.

This course covers a wide range of advanced topics in cloud computing, including cloud service models, architectures, security, networking, storage, migration strategies, cost management, DevOps practices, and emerging technologies.

What career opportunities are available for professionals with expertise in cloud computing?

Cloud computing offers a wide range of career opportunities, including roles such as cloud architect, cloud engineer, DevOps engineer, solutions architect, cloud consultant, and more, in diverse industries and organizations.
